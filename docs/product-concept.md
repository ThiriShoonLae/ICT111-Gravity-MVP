# Product Concept — Gravity

## 1. Project Title
Gravity — RSU Student Secondhand Marketplace

## 2. Target User
Primary user: RSU student **sellers** moving out under time pressure (especially graduating and international students) who must sell furniture, appliances, and textbooks within days (persona: Su Su, `/docs/user-persona.md`).
Secondary users: RSU student **buyers** looking for cheap used items at move-in, and the Gravity team **admin** who verifies accounts and moderates listings.

## 3. Problem Statement
RSU students who move out lose time and money selling used items through scattered, unsearchable Facebook and LINE groups (11/15 respondents, E01). Posts get buried within a day (E04), time pressure forces students to give away or discard working items (E02, E03 — 7/15), and contacting strangers through personal accounts feels unsafe (E05). Buyers meanwhile cannot find affordable used items in time and buy new instead.

## 4. Proposed IT Product
A web-based, mobile-friendly peer-to-peer marketplace exclusively for verified RSU students. Sellers post persistent, categorized listings once; buyers browse, search, and filter; a status system (Available / Reserved / Sold) keeps information current; contact details are revealed only after both sides agree; and an admin verifies accounts and removes invalid listings. Gravity connects the two parties — it does not hold inventory or process payments.

## 5. Core Value Proposition
**Post once, be found fast, meet safely.** One trusted searchable place replaces scattered group posts, recovers item value before sellers leave, and lets verified students make contact without exposing personal accounts.

## 6. MVP Scope
Landing page, RSU email registration with verified badge, create-listing form with validation and confirmation, listings page with search/filter/categories, listing detail view, status tracking, contact request with reveal-after-agree, admin panel (verify, moderate, correct status), dashboard summary, and mobile-responsive layout. (Full prioritization: `/docs/mvp-feature-list.md`, F01–F12.)

## 7. Out of Scope for Current Semester
- Payment processing or escrow (F15 — Gravity is a connector only)
- Natural-language AI search (F14)
- Ratings and reviews (F16)
- Storage of student ID images (verification is one-time and in person)
- Native mobile app deployment (responsive web only)
- Delivery or logistics features
